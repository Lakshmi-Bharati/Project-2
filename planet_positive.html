OUR 10 COMMITMENTS

Mahindra creates positive change, for you and the world you live in.

100% Renewable Energy

1/10

BECOMING PLANET +VE

Driving positive change in the world by going beyond carbon proactively and enabling our stakeholders to Rise.

GREENING OURSELVES

We are on an accelerated path to a sustainable, low-carbon business to reduce our environmental footprint across operations.

  • Scope 1+2 emission reduction
  • Adopting Material Circularity
  • Becoming Water Positive

M&M adopted a 60-MWp captive solar plant at Parbhani, Maharashtra to achieve 50% renewable electricity by 2025.

Energy productivity of auto sector has increased by 94% and farm sector by 87% compared to 2009.

MHRIL Kanha: India’s 1st 100% Solar Powered Resort.

Mahindra Heavy Engines Ltd. 1st Indian Company to double its energy productivity.

DECARBONIZING OUR INDUSTRIES

We are enabling our supply chain to decarbonize themselves, and pushing the industry’s shift to the use of electric vehicles & alternate fuels to

shape the future of mobility / accelerate the transition to clean mobility.

  • Supporting Net-Zero Supply Chain
  • Accelerating EV Transition
  • Auto-recycling Adoption

Supplier Code of Conduct for all M&M suppliers.

Nearly 600 suppliers, dealers and distributors trained in & assessed on sustainability.

Sustainability programs (LED lights, motor efficiency, etc.) and training with select suppliers.

REJUVENATING NATURE

We are committed to rejuvenating nature beyond our operational boundaries and enabling our communities to Rise.

  • Promoting Regenerative Agriculture
  • Afforestation at Scale
  • Investing in Green Technology
  • Watershed

Project Hariyali: 20 million trees planted since 2007, survival rate of 85%.
